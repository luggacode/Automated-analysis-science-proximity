Scientist Similarity Map

About this tool

  • Identifies scientists publishing on similar topics using OpenAlex metadata.
  • Similarity is based on publication content, not direct collaboration.
  • Geographic positions are inferred from institutional affiliations.
  • Results may be affected by missing or ambiguous author data.
  • If a person is centered in the middle of the map, the person has never been associated with any instituiton
  • Runtime depends on API response times. It might take up to 6 minutes
